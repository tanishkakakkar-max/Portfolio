Is Non-Toxic Clothing Just Another Trend
or an Actual Issue?

We obsess over what we eat to fuel our runs. Why aren't we reading the labels on what we wear?

An investigation into the chemistry of synthetic activewear - citing peer-reviewed research on endocrine disruption, microplastic shedding, and the documented gap between greenwashing claims and third-party certification standards. The Gut-Brain Connection
Your Mental Health Starts With What You Eat.

Every function in your body is connected to your gut. Your mental health is no exception.
